Philosophy: Two Different Creative Teammates

"If Seedream 4.5 is a digital illustrator, Nano Banana Pro is a DSLR camera with perfect lighting and an unlimited set of lenses."

This analogy from industry analysts captures the fundamental difference between these models. Seedream 4.5 is a stylization expert - perfect for narrative-driven, conceptual, imaginative, and design-focused visions. Nano Banana Pro is a realism expert - perfect for commercial, corporate, photographic, and product-focused visuals.

Strengths Deep Dive

Seedream 4.5 Excels At

Multi-Image Consistency

Generates multiple consecutive, highly consistent, and realistic images at once. Maintains character identity, proportions, and details across multiple outputs, angles, and actions - essential for storytelling, comic creation, and frame-by-frame creative work.

Typography and Dense Text

Poster-style key visuals with readable small text, deliberate layout, and typography-focused composition. Ideal for ads, event posters, landing pages, and brand kits. MagicBench benchmarks show designer-level composition.

Material and Texture Rendering

Accurately restores surface details such as natural creases, paper fibers, and calligraphy strokes. Realistic rendering of cloth, metal, glass with accurate reflections and physically coherent behavior.

Style Transfer and Transformation

Change images to Hong Kong 90s style, Baroque, oil painting, CG, Japanese animation, watercolor, or flat geometric styles. Transform brand textures into bronze, knitted, origami, or building block materials.

Best Use Cases

E-commerce catalogs Product mockups Game concept art Comic/storyboard creation Marketing posters Brand kits Wedding invitations

Nano Banana Pro Excels At

Infographics and Data Visualization

Turns literally anything into beautiful infographics. Can create complex multi-section posters combining dense typography, color-coded panels, structural hierarchies, and illustrative elements. Uses Google Search grounding for real-time data accuracy.

Reasoning and Logic

Built on Gemini 3 Pro, it has "great reasoning capability" for complex layouts. Can solve mathematical problems visually with correct intermediate steps. Enforces multiple simultaneous constraints: enumeration, spatial logic, and semantic filtering.

Photorealistic Detail

Images hold up against scrutiny - zoom in and they still look real. No fuzz, no mush, no digital artifacts. Razor-sharp detail, clean textures, and accurate lighting logic. Output can reach 5632x3072 pixels at approximately 24MB.

Multilingual Text Rendering

Generate text in multiple languages, localize and translate content for international scaling. Best model for creating images with correctly rendered and legible text - whether short taglines or long paragraphs.

Best Use Cases

Infographics Technical diagrams Product photography Architectural visualization Data-driven content Menu/chart design Realistic portraits

Example Outputs

To illustrate the differences between these models, here are example outputs from identical or similar prompts, demonstrating each model's strengths.

Example: Golden Hour Portrait

Prompt: "A person standing on a city rooftop during golden hour, warm sunlight wrapping around their face, subtle lens flare, wind brushing their hair, shallow depth of field, 35mm photography vibe, natural skin texture."

Seedream 4.5 Result

Produces a more stylized, cinematic interpretation with enhanced color grading and dramatic lighting. Excellent for mood-driven creative work and social media content.

Nano Banana Pro Result

Delivers camera-like realism with accurate skin textures, natural lighting physics, and true-to-life lens characteristics. Better for commercial and editorial use.

Example: Technical Infographic

Prompt: "Create an infographic about coffee brewing methods with step-by-step instructions, temperature guidelines, and timing for each method."

Seedream 4.5 Result

Creates visually appealing layouts with good typography, but may require prompt refinement for complex data hierarchies. Strong on aesthetic appeal.

Nano Banana Pro Result

Leverages reasoning to create logically structured infographics with accurate data representation, proper visual hierarchies, and semantic label placement. Industry-leading.

Example: E-commerce Product Display

Prompt: "Best-selling product display in modern e-commerce style, title 'Today's Hot Sale', main product centered, price '$9.99' below, vertical gradient background orange-pink to light purple-blue."

Seedream 4.5 Result

Excels here with precise text placement, commercial-ready layouts, and consistent product styling. Native understanding of e-commerce visual language. Clear winner for this use case.

Nano Banana Pro Result

Produces clean results with accurate text rendering, but may lean toward photorealistic product shots rather than stylized commercial layouts.

Which Model Should You Choose?

Choose Seedream 4.5 If You Need:

  • High-volume production at lower cost ($0.025-$0.04/image)
  • Multi-image consistency for comics, storyboards, or product catalogs
  • Stylized, artistic, or imaginative visuals
  • E-commerce and marketing materials with precise text placement
  • Fast turnaround (2K in 1.8 seconds)
  • Style transfer and creative transformations

Choose Nano Banana Pro If You Need:

  • Photorealistic, scrutiny-proof images
  • Infographics, diagrams, or data visualization
  • Complex reasoning and logical constraint handling
  • Multilingual text with perfect rendering
  • Real-time data integration via Google Search grounding
  • Maximum resolution output (5632x3072 at 4K)

The Hybrid Workflow (Best of Both Worlds)

Many professionals use both models strategically. A pragmatic hybrid workflow: prototype and iterate with Seedream 4.5 for its speed and cost efficiency, then finalize with Nano Banana Pro for top-tier assets requiring maximum photorealism or complex infographic layouts. For budget-sensitive projects, use Seedream 4.5 for volume work and reserve Nano Banana Pro only for hero images destined for print or paid campaigns.

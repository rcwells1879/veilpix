Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 3, 2025

Our Privacy Commitment

VeilPix is built with privacy as a core principle. We believe your images and creative work belong to you alone. This policy explains how we handle your data - spoiler: we collect almost nothing.

Image Processing

What We Do NOT Store

  • Your Images: Photos are processed in real-time and immediately discarded after editing
  • Your Prompts: Text descriptions you enter are not logged or stored
  • Editing History: Your undo/redo history exists only in your browser
  • Generated Images: Output images are delivered directly to your browser

AI Processing

VeilPix uses third-party AI models (Nano Banana Pro via Google Gemini, and SeeDream 4.0 via ByteDance) to process your images. When you submit an image for editing:

  • Your image is sent to the AI provider for processing
  • The AI provider processes your request and returns the result
  • We do not retain copies of images sent to AI providers
  • AI providers have their own privacy policies regarding temporary processing

Account Information

If you choose to create an account (via Clerk authentication), we collect:

  • Email address: For account identification and communication
  • Usage count: Number of AI editing credits used
  • Payment information: Processed securely by Stripe (we never see your full card number)

Analytics

We use Google Analytics with privacy-enhanced settings:

  • IP anonymization enabled
  • No advertising signals
  • No ad personalization
  • Limited cookie retention (30 days)

Cookies

VeilPix uses minimal cookies only for essential functionality:

  • Authentication: Session cookies if you sign in
  • Preferences: Local storage for settings (AI model selection, theme)
  • Analytics: Google Analytics cookies (anonymized)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your account data
  • Request deletion of your account
  • Export your usage history
  • Opt out of analytics

Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact us through our website.

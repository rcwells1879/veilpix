Common Mistakes to Avoid

Over-editing

Less is often more. Subtle, natural-looking edits are usually more effective than dramatic changes.

Ignoring Context

Consider the entire image when making edits. Changes should feel natural within the scene's lighting and style.

Rushing the Process

Take time to review each edit. Use the before/after preview to ensure you're improving the image.
